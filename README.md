# Power BI Sales Performance Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard analysing sales performance across multiple counties, customers, and products.  
This project highlights the use of interactive visuals and DAX measures to uncover business insights such as total sales, profitability, and transaction patterns.

---

## Overview

This report provides a comprehensive look at sales trends between **2018–2021**, allowing users to explore:

- **Total Sales** – $25.66M  
- **Total Profit** – $8.34M  
- **Total Transactions** – 10,889  
- **Total Quantity Sold** – 21,078 units  
- **Profit Margin** – 32.5%

The dashboard includes filters by **quarter** and **year**, giving flexibility to view performance over specific time periods.

---

## Report Features

### 1. **Sales by County**
A donut chart comparing revenue across top-performing counties, including Los Angeles, Orange, and San Diego.

### 2. **Profit by Date**
A time-series chart visualising profit fluctuations over several years, helping identify seasonal or yearly trends.

### 3. **Sales by Customer**
A bar chart highlighting top customers and their total sales contributions.

### 4. **Sales by Product**
Ranking of products based on sales volume, supporting product performance evaluation.

### 5. **Profit by County**
A tree map that visualises which counties contribute most to total profit.

---

## Insights

- **Los Angeles County** consistently drives the highest sales and profit.  
- **Profit margin** remains stable above 30%, indicating strong cost control.  
- Top customers account for a significant share of revenue, suggesting a concentrated customer base.  
- Profit trends reveal cyclical peaks around mid-year, useful for seasonal forecasting.

---

## Tools & Techniques

- **Power BI Desktop** – for data visualisation and dashboard creation  
- **DAX** – used for calculating KPIs (Total Sales, Profit Margin, etc.)  
- **Excel / CSV** – for initial data preparation and formatting
